Terms & Conditions

All students are strictly required to comply with the following rules. Any violation will result in a fine of Rs. 500/-, being marked absent for the next 3 days in all classes, or both, depending on the severity of the breach.

1. Computer Allotment

Computers will be assigned by the subject teacher. Students must sit only on their assigned systems and are not allowed to change seats without prior consent from the concerned teacher.

2. Prohibited Items

No eatables, chewing gum, water bottles, bags, or any similar items are allowed inside the computer lab.

3. Device Usage

Mobile phones and personal laptops are strictly not allowed inside the lab. However, laptops may be permitted in special cases with prior consent from the subject teacher. No downloading or updating of software on personal devices (laptops, mobiles, etc.) is allowed.

4. System Handling

In case of hardware or software malfunction, students must immediately report to the lab assistant or the concerned teacher. Do not attempt to fix, unplug, or tamper with any computer system, accessories, or cables.

5. Data and Software Restrictions

Do not delete, modify, or install any software or files on lab computers without permission.

6. Lab Conduct

Maintain silence and discipline at all times. Handle all equipment with care and respect others' workstations.

7. Cleanliness

Keep your workstation neat and clean before leaving. Do not leave paper, trash, or personal items in the lab. Ensure clean hands when using lab equipment; avoid touching screens or devices with dirty hands.

8. Lab Timing

Students must strictly follow their assigned lab timings and vacate the lab promptly at the end of the session.

9. Accountability

Students are responsible for the system allotted to them. Any damage or misuse will lead to disciplinary action and/or recovery of repair costs..

10. Monitoring

The lab is under CCTV surveillance. Any form of misconduct or suspicious activity will be reported and dealt with strictly.

Note: All concerned teachers must ensure strict compliance with the aforementioned rules.
